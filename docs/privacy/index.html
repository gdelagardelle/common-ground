Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Common Ground Co-Parent (“Common Ground,” “we,” “us”) is a co-parenting app for iOS. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Summary

  • Local-first: Your family data is stored on your device by default.
  • No advertising or tracking: We do not sell your data or use third-party ad trackers.
  • No account on our servers: The app does not require signing in to a Common Ground server.
  • Optional iCloud: If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in your private iCloud account via Apple CloudKit.

Information you enter

The app stores information you choose to add, such as child profiles, custody schedules, calendar events, expenses, medical notes, documents, photos, and messages between co-parents. This data remains under your control and is used only to provide app features on your device (and, if enabled, to sync between your devices or with invited co-parents via iCloud).

Data stored on your device

By default, data is saved locally using Apple’s SwiftData framework. You can protect access with Face ID or Touch ID. We do not receive copies of your family data unless you explicitly share exports (for example, a court PDF) using iOS share sheets.

Optional iCloud sync

If you turn on iCloud sync in the app, your data is stored in Apple’s CloudKit private database associated with your Apple ID. Apple’s privacy policy governs iCloud: apple.com/legal/privacy. We do not operate separate cloud servers for your content.

Optional Apple services

With your permission, the app may access:

  • Apple Calendar — to import or export events
  • Apple Health — to read height and weight for growth charts
  • Location (when in use) — to share meeting location during custody exchanges
  • Camera & Photos — to attach receipts, documents, and timeline photos
  • Face ID / Touch ID — to unlock the app
  • Notifications — for exchange reminders and alerts

You can deny or revoke these permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

On-device AI (optional)

On supported devices, the AI assistant runs on-device using Apple Intelligence. Queries are not sent to Common Ground servers. Availability depends on your device and iOS version.

Invite links

Co-parent invite links may use URLs on commonground.app or similar domains. These links identify a family invitation code; they do not upload your private family data to our servers unless a future hosted invite service is explicitly enabled and disclosed.

Analytics and crash reporting

We do not embed third-party analytics SDKs. Apple may collect anonymous crash and diagnostic data if you opt in to sharing analytics with app developers via iOS Settings.

Children’s privacy

The app is used by parents and guardians to manage information about their children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Parents are responsible for the information they enter.

Data retention and deletion

Deleting the app removes local data from your device. If iCloud sync was enabled, you may also need to remove data from iCloud via your Apple ID settings. Exported PDFs or files you save elsewhere remain under your control.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy: support@commonground.app or visit our Support page.
